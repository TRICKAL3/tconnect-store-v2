## Method 1: From Vercel Dashboard (Easiest)

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. **Look at your projects list**
3. **Find the project named:** `tconnect-store-v2` (or `tconnect-v2-0`)
4. **Click on it**
5. **You'll see the URL at the top** - it looks like:
   - `https://tconnect-v2-0-xxxxx.vercel.app`
   - Or: `https://tconnect-store-v2.vercel.app` (if you set a custom domain)

## Method 2: From Deployments

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. **Click on your frontend project**
3. **Click "Deployments"** tab
4. **Click on the latest deployment** (the one at the top)
5. **You'll see the URL** - click "Visit" or copy the URL

## Method 3: Check All Projects

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. **You should see 2 projects:**
   - `tconnect-store-v2` (or similar) - **This is your FRONTEND**
   - `tconnect-backend` (or similar) - **This is your BACKEND**
3. **Click on the FRONTEND project** (the one that's NOT "backend")
4. **The URL is shown at the top of the page**
